Reddit Repost Acceptance






This force-directed graph shows the acceptance of reposts from one subreddit to another. Upvotes on reposts causes links between nodes to turn green, while an overall number of downvotes causes the link color to turn red. Larger nodes have more original submissions than smaller nodes. Nodes receptive to reposts are colored green while those that downvote reposts are colored red. So in order to maximum the karma you get from reposts, you'll want to search the larger subreddits for content, while reposting to subreddits that are both bright green and connected to the original subreddit.

If a node is green but has a red link color, the node is receptive to reposts, but original submissions from that subreddit don't repost well to other subreddits.

The graph shows that the funny & pics are the largest creators of original content, which many subreddits feed off of without creating content that is reposted anywhere. There are many subreddits highly receptive of reposts originating from these two subreddits, such as spaceporn, chemicalreactiongifs, LifeProTips, HistoryPorn and technology. There are also subreddits to avoid if you're reposting something from funny or pics, such as ShittyPhotoshop, mspaintbattles, gaymers, NobodyCaresApostolate and ForeverAlone.

The graph shows that funny and pics are the only link to many subreddits. These gravitate towards the outside of the graph. Subreddits which get reposts from both funny AND pics end up between the two nodes, like 4chan, arresteddevelopment, WTF, GIFS, Music and StarWars. If you zoom-in(by pressing Control and +) you can see a more connected network of subreddits that feed off of each other and pics/funny, such as reactiongifs, atheism, gaming, woahdude, AdviceAnimals and movies. You may want to increase the minimum node size to 10 and the minimum upvotes to 10000 in order to see this better.

The data was obtained from Stanford's SNAP. A subset of the data can be selected manually by modifying the minimum upvotes and downvotes required for a link to be included.
